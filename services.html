Services

Translation

With a blend of meticulous research and a genuine passion for language, I ensure that your content not only accurately conveys its intended message but also flows smoothly. My primary expertise lies in English to Ukrainian translation, but don't hesitate to reach out if you require Russian translation services as well.

Editing, Proofreading and Reviewing

After the translation is complete, the next logical step is quality control to ensure consistency and eliminate errors. This is particularly important when you have more than one translator working on your project. The editor's role is to refine and perfect the translation, and I am here to provide you with precisely that level of quality.

Localization

Sometimes you need to go beyond translation. Localization involves adjusting text length, capturing the right tone, and addressing cultural nuances of your target market. I can help your app or website connect seamlessly with your Ukrainian audience.

Terminology Management

Inconsistent terminology can cause confusion and frustration. Let me assist you in standardizing your terms by building an English glossary and translating it into Ukrainian and Russian. I will work with your company's experts and respect existing term translations, preserving your brand identity.
