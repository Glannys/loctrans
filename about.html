Approach

My goal is to help your message reach your audience, keeping it clear and taking into account cultural nuances. I enjoy researching and I ask my clients questions to ensure I understand the source fully and deliver the result tailored to their needs. Another important aspect of my work is providing a translation that flows effortlessly and does not sound like a translation. I am technically savvy and happy to use CAT tools. For example, I have SDL Trados Studio 2019 and I am familiar with XTM, Phrase and Smartling. If you want to me to use another tool, this is fine too. Committed to continuous development, I always strive to be better at what I do and to grow professionally. In short, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality translations that resonate with your target audience, and I look forward to assisting you in your language needs.
