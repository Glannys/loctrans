About Me

Background

Greetings! My name is Olga Tverdomed, and I am a Ukrainian translator and localization specialist who loves language, creativity and technology.

Career highlights:
  • Bachelor’s degree in Spanish Language and Literature, Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Ukraine
  • Master’s degree in English and Spanish Language and Literature, Taras Shevchenko National University of Kyiv, Ukraine
  • Interpreted at a 2-week training for car bodyshop employees at Jaguar & Land Rover manufacturing facility in the United Kingdom
  • Worked as an editor and reviewer in a localization project of a Big Five Tech company
  • Translated numerous issues of Porsche News Magazine (covering topics such as cars, travel, lifestyle)
  • Completed two coding courses and am enrolled in the third one at the time of writing this content

Approach

My goal is to connect you with your Ukrainian audience by maintaining the clarity of your message and taking into account its cultural nuances. I enjoy conducting thorough research and I ask my clients questions to fully understand the source material and deliver results that are perfectly tailored to their needs. An important aspect of my work is providing a translation that flows naturally and doesn't sound like a translation. I am technically savvy and happy to utilize CAT tools; for example, I have SDL Trados Studio 2019 and am familiar with XTM, Phrase, and Smartling. If you'd like me to use another tool, I'm flexible and adaptable. Committed to continuous development, I always strive to enhance my skills and grow professionally. In short, I am dedicated to delivering high-quality translations that resonate with your target audience, and I look forward to assisting you with your language needs.
